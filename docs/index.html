About

Started in 2018 by Joshua Schmidt and Krishanu, Locnet is an intuitive, interactive mobile social-network platform. It is open-source, and encourages users to pursue in-person interaction as opposed to through the medium of the app.

To accomplish this, the platform is entirely based on location data, so friend groups can see where each other are to meet in person have fun. Being open-source, Locnet encourages users to inspect the code, to see that their data is not being misused. The user data is protected using blockchain.

Story

After seeing all of the problems with conventional social networks, and recognizing the potential in creating a completely new platform, we decided to do just that. The concept was to create a completely open platform that stresses meeting and connecting with new people, and stressing in-person interactions. The entire platform is location-based, meaning users can see in real-time if their friends are nearby and can then meet up with them. Nearby events, activities, shops, businesses, and attractions are shown in a personalized user-feed.
